1. Summer McIntosh
Summer McIntosh is a world record holder and has won many medals. At just 17 years old, she has three Olympic gold medals and one silver from the 2024 Games. She excels in events like the 200m and 400m individual medley and the 200m butterfly, where she set a world record time of 4:24.38 in the 400m individual medley.
2. Pan Zhanle
Pan Zhanle holds the world record for the 100-meter freestyle, with an impressive time of 46.40 seconds. He is the first Chinese swimmer to swim under 22 seconds in the 50-meter freestyle, under 47 seconds in the 100-meter freestyle, and under 1:45 in the 200-meter freestyle.
3. Leon Marchand
Leon Marchand holds the world record in the 400m individual medley in a time of 4:02.50, breaking Michael Phelps' long-standing record. His achievements and potential make him one of the sport's brightest stars.
4. Regan Smith
Regan Smith holds world records in backstroke and excels in butterfly and medley events. Her high VO2 max helps her swim fast and recover quickly.
5. Ryan Lochte
Ryan Lochte has won 12 Olympic medals, including 6 golds, and holds multiple World Championship titles. He owns the current world record in the 200m individual medley with a time of 1:54.00.

6. Kristof Milak
Kristóf Milák holds the world record in the 200m butterfly and has won multiple gold medals at the World Championships and the Olympics.
7. Michael Phelps
Michael Phelps is the most decorated Olympian in history, with 28 Olympic medals, including 23 golds. He was dominant in butterfly, medley, and freestyle relay events.
8. Kaylee McKeown
Kaylee McKeown holds world records in the 50m and 200m backstroke and is the first woman in nearly 50 years to win both backstroke events in two consecutive Olympics.
9. Katie Ledecky
Katie Ledecky is known for her dominance in long-distance freestyle events. She holds world records in the 800m and 1500m freestyle.
10. Caeleb Dressel
Caeleb Dressel is known for his incredible power and speed. He holds world records in the 100m butterfly and has won multiple Olympic gold medals.
